Hello! This bot combines the convenience of Telegram, the power of ChatGPT, and the fun of Tinder.

The chatbot can:

Generate a Tinder profile based on a description 😎
Write interesting and intriguing messages for introductions. 😘
Engage in conversations on your behalf 😇🔥
You can practice conversations with the chatbot. 🥰
Useful commands and links:

/start — bot's main menu
/profile — generate a Tinder profile 😎
/opener — message for introductions 🥰
/message — conversation on your behalf 😈
/date — chat with celebrities 🔥
/gpt - ask a question to the GPT chat 🧠
